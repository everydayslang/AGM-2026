The World Ahead of the AI Age

September 24, 2026  ·  8:30 AM – 5:00 PM  ·  Peak at Hudson Yards

Hype has calmed down, AI is widespread, integrating the tech into the society, how world changes when AI is everywhere

Most of the conversation in our industry lives inside the AI hype cycle. This year, we want to push past it. Once the hype settles and AI is woven into the broader fabric of society, what does that world actually look like — and how do we navigate it? Across the day, leading founders, investors, and government leaders will explore that question through four lenses: the public markets, applications and agents, infrastructure (including energy, chips, and compute), and AI's impact on the economy and labor market.

Location and Venue

Peak at Hudson Yards

30 Hudson Yards, 101st Floor

New York, New York 10001

In the heart of Hudson Yards, Peak is a special venue set above the clouds on the 101st floor of 30 Hudson Yards. Peak brings stunning views of New York City, positioned at the summit of the 1,296-foot tall tower. Peak sits one level above Edge, the highest observation deck in the Western Hemisphere.

Instructions for Entry to Peak at Hudson Yards

  1. Please use the entrance at the Shops at Hudson Yards across from the Vessel.
  2. You will check in at Quin Bar, located on the 5th floor of The Shops at Hudson Yards, accessible via the first-floor elevators adjacent to Cartier.
  3. Please note that escalators and other elevators do not provide access to the 5th floor. Please leave a few extra minutes to get to the venue as there are a few check-in points before arriving at the 101st floor.
